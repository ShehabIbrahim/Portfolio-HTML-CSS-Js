NJFit:
Augmenting Recreational Group Activities


What was this project?

Our Vision:

We aim to promote healthy living and create a closer community within NJIT by bringing people to the gym. With the new addition of the Wellness Center this semester, we plan to propose a way to get students more involved on campus while also improving their overall health and fitness.

Problem We Were Addressing:

Lack of community and athletic involvement within NJIT caused by the absence of opportunities given to students and social events, communities, clubs, and other organizations.

Method We Used to Address and Solve the Problem:

Storytelling Design

Our Rationale for Using this Method:

Storytelling Design enables us to understand the context of our potential users through the creation of data-driven stories. Through this method, we analyze the journey that these users go through leading to the requirements of a system that could address their pain points and improve their journey.
